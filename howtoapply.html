How To Apply For A Grant

Due to the resources available to the Anthony F. Cordeiro Charitable Foundation, we can only fund a small percentage of the grant requests we receive. Only completed applications will be reviewed.

Programs of Interest:

  • -Social service organizations, particularly for children and families, the disabled, the elderly, and homeless
  • -Educational programs

Geographical:

  • -The Anthony F. Cordeiro Charitable Foundation was incorporated to support the communities of southeastern Massachusetts and Rhode Island.

The following entities are ineligible for a grant from the Foundation:

  • -Individuals
  • -Political organizations
  • -Fraternal organizations
  • -Organizations that are not classified as a 501 (c) (3) by the IRS

Application Procedure:

To be considered for funding, you must complete our online application. Be sure to provide all the required documentation as indicated on the form instructions. All requested supporting documentation will need to be uploaded.

If you are new to our online application, you will be prompted to create a username and password before beginning.

If you are unable to complete the application in one-sitting, please save it and return to it later.

Once your application is complete, please press the submit button located at the bottom of the application, which will deliver your application.

Deadline:

There is no deadline to submit an application. All grant requests are reviewed on a rolling basis. If funded, applicants will receive a response by mail.
