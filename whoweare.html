Who We Are

The Anthony F. Cordeiro Charitable Foundation aims to provide funds to non-profit organizations in the communities of southeastern Massachusetts and selected areas of Rhode Island.

The Foundation’s mission is to strengthen the community by enhancing opportunities for:

  • Access to Healthcare
  • Social Service Organizations
  • Education

It is our goal to provide financial assistance to causes and organizations that make a direct impact on the lives of local individuals and families who live in our shared communities.

Communities we serve:

Selected communities within southeastern Massachusetts and Rhode Island.
